# Timber International - B2B Brand Narrative
**Created:** December 14, 2025
**Purpose:** Repositioning from B2C artisan brand to B2B industrial timber supplier

---

## Brand Transformation Overview

### FROM: B2C Artisan Positioning
- Emotional, lifestyle-focused messaging
- Individual consumer appeal
- "Passion," "vision," "crafted with love"
- Discovery and exploration language

### TO: B2B Industrial Positioning
- Reliability and consistency focused
- Business partnership emphasis
- Specifications, certifications, capacity
- Supply chain and logistics language

---

## Core Messaging Framework

### Value Propositions
1. **Consistency at Scale**: Predictable quality across high-volume orders
2. **Technical Precision**: Specifications you can build your business on
3. **Partnership Model**: Your success is our success
4. **Sustainable Operations**: Certified, compliant, future-proof

### Key Statistics to Emphasize
- Production capacity (units/month)
- Quality rejection rates (<1%)
- On-time delivery performance (99%+)
- Years in business
- Certifications held (FSC, ISO, etc.)
- Repeat customer rate

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Do Use:
- Direct, factual statements
- Specific metrics and numbers
- Industry terminology
- "Partner," "reliability," "specifications"
- Performance data
- Certifications and standards

### Don't Use:
- "Passion," "dream," "vision"
- "Beautiful," "elegant," "stunning"
- "Discover," "explore," "journey"
- Emotional appeals
- Lifestyle imagery
